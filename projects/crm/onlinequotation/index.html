Online Quotation System

Turning a manual, slow quotation process into a self-serve system that scaled with growth.

The problem

During rapid growth, installation partners relied on spreadsheets and phone calls. Quotes took days, customers dropped off, and revenue was leaking at the most critical moment.

My approach

Instead of waiting for a long product roadmap, I mapped the bottlenecks, reused existing tools, and shipped a lightweight self-serve quotation flow that partners could adopt immediately.

Impact
<1 hr Quote turnaround
96% Faster lead time
$5M Revenue unlocked
0 Customer complaints

This system became the foundation for scaling installation services without adding headcount.
